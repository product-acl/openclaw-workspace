# Social Media Management Tools — Organic + Paid Comparison

> Last updated: 2026-03-07

## Platform Coverage + Upload Capabilities

| Tool | Organic | Google Ads | Facebook Ads | Reddit Ads | Apple Search Ads | Upload Organic | Upload Paid | Both |
|------|---------|------------|--------------|------------|-----------------|---------------|-------------|------|
| **Metricool** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Hootsuite** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ (boost only) | ⚠️ |
| **SocialBee** | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ (basic) | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ (reports only) | ❌ |
| **Sprout Social** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ (reports only) | ❌ |
| **AdEspresso** | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ |
| **Madgicx** | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ |

## Key

- ✅ **Both** = create + publish organic posts AND create + launch paid campaigns from one dashboard
- ⚠️ **Boost only** = can boost existing posts but not build full campaigns from scratch
- ❌ **Reports only** = connects to ad platform for analytics but requires native dashboard to create ads

## Notes

- **No tool covers Reddit Ads + Apple Search Ads alongside organic.** These require native dashboards.
- **Metricool** is the only tool that genuinely does both organic and full paid campaign creation across multiple platforms from one place.
- For solo operators (like SpinUp launch): use Metricool for organic + Google/Meta ads, run Reddit Ads and Apple Search Ads natively.
