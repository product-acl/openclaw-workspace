# SpinUp Visual Assets Brief

## App Icon
- **Concept**: A spinning card or vortex that represents both "spinning" (party game mechanic) and "spinning up" a relationship. Dual-color gradient shows the two modes merging.
- **Style**: Minimalist, geometric, premium feel with subtle glow effects. Should look sophisticated but playful—not childish or clipart-like.
- **Colors**: Primary #7C3AFF (party purple) fading to #FF3D6B (couples rose) on dark bg #0E0E1A. Gradient should be smooth and visually appealing at small sizes.
- **Must work at 1024×1024 and small sizes (60×60)**: Clean lines, good contrast, recognizable silhouette even when tiny.

## App Store Screenshots (6 required for iOS)
1. **Hero shot** — Show both modes side-by-side: left side shows party scene with friends laughing, right shows couple cozy on couch. Overlay: "Two games in one app"
2. **Party Mode feature** — Close-up of phone showing a "spicy" drinking card with "Undo" button visible. Background: blurred party scene.
3. **Couples Mode feature** — Phone showing relationship challenge card with scoring/points visible. Background: romantic candlelit setting.
4. **IAP / Unlock screen** — Clean screen showing "$4.99 • Unlock Everything • No Subscription" prominently. Comparison to competitors' subscription prices.
5. **Card examples** — Grid of 4-6 different card types: drinking challenge, truth question, dare, relationship prompt. Shows variety.
6. **Social proof / vibe shot** — Collage of user scenarios: college party, date night at home, game night with friends, couple traveling. Text: "Your game night, upgraded"

## Social Media Templates
- **Instagram square (1080×1080)**: Launch announcement concept with app icon centered, gradient background, text: "SpinUp is here 🎉 Two games in one app. No subscription. Link in bio!"
- **Instagram Story (1080×1920)**: "Game night just got better" concept showing phone mockup with card animation, swipe gesture arrow, tagline: "Swipe. Play. Connect. Download now."

## Style Direction
- **Dark, premium, playful — not childish**: Think premium gaming app, not cartoonish party game. Sophisticated color palette with pops of vibrant purple/pink.
- **Card-game aesthetic but modern (no clipart)**: Clean card designs with subtle shadows, rounded corners, but avoid looking like physical playing cards. Digital-first aesthetic.
- **Typography-forward (Poppins-based)**: Use Poppins family consistently. Bold for headlines, medium for body. Good hierarchy and spacing.
- **Motion where appropriate**: Subtle animations for card reveals, spins, transitions. Smooth, not jarring.
- **Real photography for backgrounds**: Use authentic-looking party/couple scenes (stock or staged) with proper blur/overlay treatment. Avoid cheesy stock photos.
