# SpinUp Positioning Doc

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
### Party Mode ICP
- **Demographics**: 21-35 year olds, college students and young professionals, urban/suburban
- **Psychographics**: Social, extroverted, value experiences over possessions, tech-savvy, enjoy casual gaming
- **Behavior**: Host or attend weekend gatherings, use smartphones for entertainment, willing to spend $5-10 for quality apps, frustrated by subscription models

### Couples Mode ICP
- **Demographics**: 25-45 year olds, in committed relationships (dating, engaged, married), dual-income households
- **Psychographics**: Relationship-focused, value quality time, willing to invest in relationship growth, prefer meaningful experiences over passive entertainment
- **Behavior**: Have regular "date nights" at home, use relationship apps occasionally, frustrated by expensive subscriptions for simple card decks

## The Problem We Solve

**Party Mode**: Most drinking game apps are either too expensive (subscription-based), too repetitive, or lack real game mechanics. Players want spontaneous fun without financial commitment anxiety or feeling nickel-and-dimed.

**Couples Mode**: Existing couples apps are either clinical therapy tools or shallow question dumps with aggressive subscription models. Couples want playful, meaningful connection that feels like a game—not homework—without recurring payments.

## Our Solution

SpinUp delivers premium card game experiences for both parties and couples with a one-time purchase model. Party mode offers drinking games with real mechanics (not just card draws), while couples mode provides relationship-building games that feel playful, not clinical. Both modes live in one app, letting users switch contexts without downloading separate apps.

## Differentiators
1. **No subscription — ever ($4.99 one-time)** - Unlike competitors charging $7-40/month
2. **Undo button (party mode)** - Fix accidental swipes or "too spicy" moments
3. **Real game mechanics (couples mode)** - Not just question dumps; actual gameplay with scoring, challenges, and progression
4. **Two audiences, one download** - Party and couples modes in a single app with shared premium unlock
